About


Quake2Lithium.com is a fansite and community born out of a simple idea; an international Quake 2 Lithium revival. 

Without the hard work and support of others, this site would not be possible. 

Special thanks to:
  • Jouni Rajala for designing the theme and some graphical elements of this site.
  • Flagship Industries for sponsoring Ventrilo access for this site.
  • Matt "WhiteFang" Ayres for creating and releasing as open source, the Lithium Mod.
  • All those playing for keeping Lithium alive all these years!
  • The larger Quake II community for their various contributions.
