Welcome to Quake2Lithium.com!


Quake2Lithium.com is a fansite and international community dedicated to preserving the legacy of Lithium.  Lithium is a server-side modification for Quake 2 created by Matt "WhiteFang" Ayres and released as open source in January 2010.  Quake 2 Lithium is easily one of the fastest first person shooters ever released with unique gameplay including an off-hand grappling hook and runes (power-ups).  See the action for yourself!  Ready to play? Read our Quick Start Guide and with a FREE download, you're all set to frag!
