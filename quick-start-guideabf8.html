Step 1

Download and extract R1Q2 windows client.



Step 2

Install the map pak!



Step 3

Setup your autoexec.cfg and add Lithium II specific bindings. Your autoexec.cfg file should be placed in your baseq2 folder.

For advanced configs, see here.

// Lithium Specific -- change the key bindings to what you like...
bind SPACE +hook
bind tab +scores
bind g "drop rune"
bind v "chaselist"
bind , "observe"
bind . "chaseleader"
bind p "vote"

Step 4

Come and play!!! Start up using r1q2.exe and type connect server:port in console.  Check our Servers section for a list of Lithium servers with hostname or IP, and port.

Runes (appear as pyramid shaped entities):

  • Strength (Red) You inflict 2x damage
  • Resist (Blue) You take 1/2 damage
  • Haste (Yellow) You fire twice as fast
  • Regen (Green) Your health regenerates slowly
  • Vampire (Purple) You gain health for damage inflicted

The command "drop rune" in the console will cause you to drop the rune you are currently carrying. You can pick up the rune that you have just dropped.
